Latitude vs. Cloudiness

This Plot is using the Latitude as the x-axis and plots it against the cloudiness percentage of given dataset. The data includes 500 cities chossen at random.

Now looking at the data at hand in more detail, we are able to reveal severeal Obseravtions. For one, there are 3 major clusters. One of these clusters is seen, ranging from 40-60 degree latitude at roughly 90% cloudiness. A second predominant cluster can be found at 0% cloudiness mark and stretches from -50 to 70 degree on the latitude scale. These very interesting insights do not bring us any close to a real revelation as to if this data visualisation is actually useful.
